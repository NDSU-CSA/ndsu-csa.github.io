HACKCLUB INFORMATION HUB

The NDSU Cybersecurity Student Association (aka HackClub) is a peer-learning organization focused on teaching cybersecurity skills to both club members and the general populace. This is accomplished by weekly, student lead meetings on various cyber topics; and participation in cybersecurity competitions and conferences. Though we do regularly participate in competitions, we are not a competitive organization, so participation is optional (but highly recommended for its learning value!).

If you would like to join or have any questions feel free to reach out via the links below!
